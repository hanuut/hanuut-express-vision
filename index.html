Hanuut Express Project

The strategic vision, operational roadmap, and central documentation hub for building the digital backbone of local commerce in Algeria.

Our Vision: The Digital Mall for Algeria

The Algerian market presents a unique and significant opportunity. There is a critical lack of integrated digital platforms for local businesses, who are forced to use fragmented, inefficient methods for inventory, sales, and delivery.

Our mission is to become the indispensable "digital mall" in every Algerian's phone. We are not just building apps; we are building a full-stack commerce ecosystem that creates a seamless loop between shops, customers, and drivers. We manage the storefront, the back-office, and the last-mile logistics—something no competitor currently offers in an integrated way.

The Hanuut Express Ecosystem

Our platform is a suite of three interconnected applications, powered by a robust central API, each designed for a specific user.

Hanuut (For Customers)

A safe, reliable, and user-friendly online marketplace to discover and order from trusted local shops with real-time tracking.

My Hanuut (For Shop Owners)

A powerful business tool that allows any shop to create an online store, digitize inventory, and process online orders seamlessly.

Tawsila (For Logistics Agents)

A flexible platform for drivers and agents to earn income by fulfilling delivery requests and providing ride-sharing services.

Strategic Roadmap

Our growth is structured in deliberate, measurable phases. Here's our journey from a validated concept to a market-defining platform.

Phase 0: Foundation & Validation DONE

We have moved beyond the idea phase and validated our model in a real-world environment, proving the technology and market need.

  • Technology MVP: Developed and tested Minimum Viable Products for all three applications (Hanuut, My Hanuut, Tawsila), the backend API, and a management dashboard.
  • Market Testing: Completed two years of iterative testing in Arris, Batna (a 40,000-person market), gathering crucial user feedback.
  • Initial Partnerships: Successfully onboarded our first three shops (2 food, 1 grocery) and built a pipeline of future partners.

Phase 1: Market Penetration & Refinement IN PROGRESS

This is our current focus: perfecting the operational model, expanding our local partner base, and refining the product based on our go-to-market strategy.

Key Objectives:

  • Partner Expansion: Actively onboarding diverse shop types including a butchery, a fruits & vegetables shop, a large grocery warehouse, two restaurants, and two bakeries.
  • E-commerce Enabler: Positioning "My Hanuut" as the go-to solution for non-tech-savvy individuals and small brands to get a professional online shop and a shareable link for social media.
  • Product Enhancement:
    • Hanuut App: Developing recommendation algorithms for the home screen and improving the overall user experience.
    • My Hanuut App: Adapting the UX for specific user roles (Owner vs. Worker) to ensure operational clarity and traceability.
    • Web Platform: Adding more customization options (colors, content) for our partners' public-facing web pages.
    • Payments: Integrating and rigorously testing the SATIM payment gateway to enable secure online payments.
  • Marketing & Branding: Supporting our partners with a strong online presence by creating at least six high-quality reels per week to feature their businesses and market our platform.
  • Logistics Foundation: Beginning controlled testing of Tawsila with a select group of delivery agents while developing a unified login system for Hanuut users to seamlessly access Tawsila.

Phase 2: Regional Expansion & Monetization NEXT

Leveraging our perfected model to expand into new cities and activate our primary revenue streams.

  • Geographic Growth: Expand partnerships across major regional hubs including Batna, Setif, and Constantine.
  • Revenue Model: Focus on activating and scaling a transaction commission-based revenue model.

Phase 3: Scale & Diversification FUTURE

Becoming the undisputed market leader by scaling all operations and introducing new, high-value services for our partners and users.

  • Advanced Partner Tools: Introduce premium subscription plans for shops seeking advanced analytics, inventory tools, and marketing features.
  • Advertising Platform: Launch integrated advertising products to help shops increase their visibility within the Hanuut ecosystem.
  • Tawsila as a Logistics Platform: Evolve Tawsila into a comprehensive logistics and transportation service.
    • Offer on-demand transport for packages (C2C) and personal rides.
    • Support a wide range of vehicles: scooters, bicycles, cars, vans, and trucks.
    • Introduce missions for agents without vehicles, such as warehouse management, order preparation, or short-distance walking deliveries.
